About

Luong Vuong Nguyen has been a Senior Lecturer and Researcher at the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence, FPT University, Da Nang, Vietnam, since October 2022. He has also served as an Adjunct Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at The Catholic University of Korea since November 2022. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Chung-Ang University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2022, and previously completed his M.S. in Computer Science at The University of Danang – University of Technology.
